What is Finance?

Finance is the management of money, investments, and financial activities.

What is Investment?

Investment is the process of allocating money to assets or ventures to generate returns.

Why Invest?

Investing helps in wealth growth, financial security, and achieving long-term financial goals.

Importance of Money

Money is crucial for fulfilling needs, improving living standards, and ensuring financial stability.

Categories of Investment

Investments are classified into stocks, bonds, real estate, commodities, and mutual funds.
